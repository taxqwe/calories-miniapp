BMR and PAL Calculator

BMR — basal metabolic rate, calories needed to maintain basic body functions at rest. i BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body burns at rest over 24 hours to maintain vital functions (breathing, circulation, brain function, etc.).

PAL (or PAR) — physical activity ratio, a factor accounting for daily physical activities. i PAL (Physical Activity Level) or PAR (Physical Activity Ratio) is a multiplier that shows how much additional energy is required for your daily activities. PAL/PAR combined with BMR gives your total daily calorie requirement.
